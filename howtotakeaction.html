Renewable Energy

Examples of renewable energy sources include wind power, solar power, bioenergy (organic matter burned as a fuel) and hydroelectric, including tidal energy

Public Transportation

Public transportation gets people where they're going while emitting far fewer climate-warming greenhouse gases than private cars.

Recyling

An example of external recycling is the collection of old newspapers and magazines for repulping and their manufacture into new paper products.
